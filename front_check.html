Frequently Asked Questions

  • What documents should I bring?

    Bring any correspondence, contracts, identification, court documents, or other paperwork related to your legal issue.

  • How quickly can I speak to a solicitor?

    In most cases, we can arrange a consultation within one business day. Urgent criminal defence matters are available 24/7.

  • Can you represent me anywhere in the UK?

    Yes. We represent clients throughout England and Wales, with many consultations conducted remotely by phone or video call.

  • Do you offer fixed-fee legal services?

    Yes. Many of our services are available with transparent fixed pricing, so you know exactly what to expect.

  • How much does an initial consultation cost?

    We offer fixed-fee initial consultations for many legal services. The fee depends on the type and complexity of your matter.
